# Xiaohongshu Cover — Notion Style

Create a Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) cover image in Notion minimalist hand-drawn style.

## Canvas
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait)
- Background: warm off-white #FAFAFA with subtle paper texture

## Layout: Sparse
Large visual impact. Main illustration takes center stage with generous whitespace.

## Visual Elements
- Center illustration: A pipeline diagram — 3 connected boxes labeled "规划 Plan" → "配置 Config" → "执行 Execute", hand-drawn style with arrows
- Around the pipeline: 3 small floating badge cards, hand-drawn rectangles labeled: "隔离" / "幂等" / "可观测" — like sticky notes
- Top: Large bold title text
- Bottom: Subtitle and a small hand-drawn checkmark badge

## Typography
- Main title line 1 (large, bold, black #1A1A1A): AI 工程师的
- Main title line 2 (large, bold): 11 条原则
- Subtitle (medium): 造 AI 系统时，每个坑都踩过一次
- Small tag at bottom: 开源 Claude Code Skill 🔗

## Style Details
- Notion minimalist: clean warm-white background
- Hand-drawn wobble effect on all elements
- Single-weight ink lines, dark charcoal #37352F
- Blue accent #4A90E2 for pipeline arrows and highlights
- Pastel yellow highlight behind the title "11 条"
- Small hand-drawn underline under "11 条原则"
- Generous whitespace, airy feel

## Overall Feel
Intellectual, clean, slightly technical — like an engineering notebook page turned into a cover card.
