# Composition
Type composition:
- Scene type: Atmospheric dark terminal environment with pixel ghost companion sitting beside the code window

Visual composition:
- Main visual: A dark terminal/code editor window open at night, showing green monospace code text. A cute pixel-art ghost (semi-transparent, glowing softly in white/blue) floats beside the terminal, looking at the screen with curiosity. The ghost has simple rounded pixel features — two dark pixel eyes, a slight smile.
- Layout: Terminal window takes up most of the composition (left and center), ghost character sits in the lower-right area, slightly overlapping the terminal edge. The scene should feel intimate and cozy, like a late-night coding session.
- Background: Very dark navy/black background (#0D1117 or similar). Subtle grid or scanline texture.
- Decorative: Faint neon green glow from the terminal illuminating the ghost. Small floating pixel particles or sparkles around the ghost. A faint speech bubble near the ghost showing "..." or a small cursor blink symbol.
- Title text placement: Top portion of the image, in bold display font, white or neon green, pixel-style lettering.

Color scheme:
- Background: Deep dark #0D1117 (GitHub dark style)
- Terminal glow: Neon green #00FF41 (Matrix green)
- Ghost: Pure white #FFFFFF with soft blue-white glow, semi-transparent
- Accent: Electric blue #4FC3F7 for particle effects
- Title text: Bright white or lime green

Rendering notes: Full pixel art aesthetic. Hard pixel edges, no anti-aliasing. 16-bit or 32-bit game sprite style. Chunky pixels. The ghost should look like a classic video game sprite. The terminal should have a pixel-art border/frame.

Type notes: Scene type — atmospheric, narrative, mood-setting. The viewer should feel they are looking through a window at a programmer's late-night coding session, with a small companion present.

Palette notes: Dark palette — deep shadows, high contrast neon accents, minimal mid-tones. The darkness makes the terminal glow and ghost luminescence dramatic and striking.
