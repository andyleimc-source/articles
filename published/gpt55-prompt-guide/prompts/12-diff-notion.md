# 04 · Notion 风：硬规则封路 vs 决策路标分叉

A **Notion-style flat illustration**, **16:9 aspect ratio**, editorial concept diagram.

## Style anchors

- Flat 2D vector illustration with soft hand-drawn outline imperfection
- Soft pastel palette: cream `#F5EDE0` background sky, **muted dusty rose `#E8A8A0`**, **soft sage green `#A8C8A0`**, **warm grey `#9DA8B0`**, accent **gentle gold `#D4A862`**
- Subtle paper grain
- Generous whitespace
- Friendly + slightly contemplative mood

## Composition

A wide flat illustration of a peaceful path through gentle rolling hills, **divided into two halves** by a soft vertical break.

**Left half — "the old way" (slightly cooler tones):**
- A **stone wall** built of stacked rectangular blocks **blocking the path**
- Each block is illustrated flat-style with slight texture; **only 4 blocks** in total visible, each with a single uppercase word carved in a clean sans-serif:
  - `ALWAYS`
  - `NEVER`
  - `MUST`
  - `CRITICAL`
- The wall feels heavy but not menacing — more like an old brick fence than a fortress
- A small thought-bubble near the wall containing a tiny `?` mark (subtle, optional)
- The path ends abruptly at the wall

**Right half — "the new way" (slightly warmer tones):**
- A **wooden signpost** at a Y-shaped fork in the path
- Two wooden arrow boards on the post, each clearly legible:
  - Left arrow board: `if A →`
  - Right arrow board: `if B →`
- Two paths visibly diverge from the post and gently wind into the distance
- The horizon is open and inviting

**Background environment (whole image):**
- Soft rolling hills in the distance, drawn in flat shapes with a few simple gradient bands (cream → soft peach → muted sage)
- A subtle sun shape (just a flat circle, no glow) in the upper-right
- A few tiny cloud shapes (flat, simple)
- Sparse decoration: small dots, tiny + marks, a single tiny squiggle — Notion-illustration accents, very minimal

**Bottom caption strip (subtle banner across the bottom edge):**
- A clean horizontal beige strip with the text: `Decision rules > Absolute rules`
- Single line, sans-serif, gentle dark grey, perfectly legible

## Strict rules

- **Flat illustration only.** No photography. No 3D. No realistic stone or wood textures.
- No people, no animals, no robots, no vehicles, no plants/trees (replace with cloud/hill shapes for atmosphere)
- No purple gradients, no neon, no glow effects
- All text — `ALWAYS / NEVER / MUST / CRITICAL`, `if A →`, `if B →`, and bottom caption — must be clean, sharp, perfectly legible
- No additional words anywhere in the illustration

## Mood

A calm, almost philosophical contrast. The wall feels finite and old; the signpost feels inviting and adaptive. Like Notion's "we built two paths for you" type illustrations — gentle but pointed.
