# 03 · Notion 风：四代 prompt 厚度演化

A **Notion-style flat illustration**, **16:9 aspect ratio**, editorial diagram image.

## Style anchors

- Flat 2D vector illustration, soft hand-drawn outline imperfection
- Soft pastel palette: **cream `#F5EDE0`** background, **dusty brown `#8B6F4E`**, **soft sky blue `#B8D4E0`**, **muted peach `#F0C9A8`**, **warm beige `#D9C9B0`**, accent **gentle gold `#D4A862`**
- Subtle paper grain texture
- Lots of whitespace
- Friendly, calm, slightly playful — like Notion's marketing illustrations

## Composition

A horizontal flat-lay illustration of a desk, top-down view. **Four objects arranged left to right** on a cream paper background, each progressively smaller / thinner / newer-looking. They sit on an implied light-grey baseline.

**Object 1 (leftmost) — 2021:**
- A **very thick, old hardcover manual**, illustrated flat-style, brown weathered cover
- A few squiggly age-marks above
- Tiny year tag below: `2021`

**Object 2 — 2023:**
- A **medium-thick clear binder** with visible inserted pages, soft sky-blue cover
- Pages inside hint at small angle-bracket marks `<>` `</>` in pale ink (don't make them look like real code, just decorative)
- Year tag below: `2023`

**Object 3 — 2024:**
- A **thin stack of A4 papers**, clipped together with a small gold paper clip
- A tiny red round stamp visible on top sheet (just a circle, no readable text inside)
- Year tag below: `2024`

**Object 4 (rightmost) — 2026:**
- A **single yellow sticky note**, slightly curled corner, clearly the lightest object
- One short line of hand-written text on it: `Translate to French.` (this is the only long text in the image — must be legible, no other readable text needed)
- Year tag below: `2026`

**Connecting flow:**
- A subtle hand-drawn arrow `→` between each pair of objects (cream/grey, slight curve)

**Decoration:**
- A few tiny floating accent shapes (small dots, tiny + signs, a couple of squiggly lines) sprinkled lightly around the composition — Notion-illustration accents, sparing
- A horizontal density bar at the bottom: 4 segments, decreasing in width left to right (thick rust → narrow gold)
- Below the bar, in tiny grey hand-lettered text: `getting thinner →` (single short line, no Chinese/English bilingual mash)

**Title (top of image, optional small label):**
- Small clean sans-serif label top-left in muted grey: `Prompt evolution` (small, secondary)

## Strict rules

- **Flat illustration only.** No photography. No realistic textures. No 3D.
- No screens, computers, robots, people, AI mascots
- No code text in any object except the sticky note's `Translate to French.`
- No purple gradients, no neon, no glowing effects
- All visible text must be perfectly legible (no garbled glyphs)
- Year tags must be clean: just `2021` `2023` `2024` `2026`

## Mood

Calm, observational, mildly nostalgic. Like a Notion blog header illustration showing the lineage of a tool. The thinning trend should feel gentle and inevitable, not dramatic.
