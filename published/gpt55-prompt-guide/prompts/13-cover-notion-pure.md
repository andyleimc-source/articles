# 封面 v4：纯正 Notion vector 插画风

## Visual reference

A flat vector illustration in the EXACT style of Notion's official marketing illustrations, Notion's homepage hero graphics, and Notion's docs cover images. Think also of: Stripe.com illustrations, Mailchimp's brand illustrations, Linear.app's marketing visuals.

**Critical style anchor: pure flat vector, NO painterly textures, NO watercolor wash, NO photographic grain, NO realistic lighting.** This must look like an SVG, not a painting.

## Image spec

- **Aspect**: 16:9
- **Background**: solid soft cream-paper color `#FAF6F0`, completely flat (no gradient, no texture)

## Composition

A clean, minimalist flat illustration with two contrasting handbook objects sitting side by side on a horizontal beige baseline. Generous whitespace above and around them.

**Left object — "the old way" (smaller, slightly faded, secondary):**
- A flat-vector illustration of a thick old book
- Solid muted brown fill `#A88671`, with a darker brown spine `#7A5C4A`
- Just a handful of clean horizontal lines on the spine for spine detail (NOT a realistic leather texture)
- Two simple beige rectangular tabs sticking out the top edge
- Optional: 2-3 tiny squiggle marks `~` `~` floating above to suggest age/dust (clean linework only)
- No detailed text on the cover — leave it blank or with a single thin horizontal line indicating a title

**Center — connector:**
- A simple, slightly hand-drawn-style **arrow** `→` between the two books, in soft warm grey `#9A8B7E`
- Single curved stroke, no embellishment

**Right object — "the new way" (the focal point, brighter, slightly larger and more prominent):**
- A flat-vector illustration of a slim, modern softcover handbook
- Solid creamy off-white fill `#FFFCF5` with a thin warm grey outline `#C9B89E`
- Centered on its cover: a single large **circular gold seal** in solid gold `#D4A862`, with a subtle scalloped edge (one ring of small triangular notches around the perimeter)
- **Inside the gold seal: just the numeral `7`** in bold white sans-serif (must be perfectly legible)
- **NO Chinese characters anywhere on the booklet** — leave the booklet's lower area blank
- Below the seal, a single thin warm-grey horizontal line (suggesting a subtitle is there, but no actual text)

**Decoration (very sparse, Notion-illustration accents):**
- 1 tiny solid gold dot `•` to the upper-right of the new booklet
- 1 small four-pointed sparkle `✦` (flat solid gold, simple) to the right
- 1 tiny `+` mark in soft warm grey somewhere in the negative space
- A thin horizontal beige line `#E8DFCE` running across the lower third of the image (suggesting a desk surface)
- That's it — no other decoration

## Color palette (use ONLY these)

- Background cream: `#FAF6F0`
- Old book brown: `#A88671` (fill), `#7A5C4A` (darker accents)
- New booklet cream: `#FFFCF5` (fill), `#C9B89E` (outline)
- Gold seal: `#D4A862`
- Soft warm grey accents: `#9A8B7E`
- Subtle baseline: `#E8DFCE`
- **NO other colors.** Especially NO purple, NO blue, NO green, NO red.

## Strict prohibitions

- **NO painterly textures** — no watercolor wash, no oil paint feel, no charcoal grain
- **NO photo-realistic lighting** — no glossy highlights, no soft shadows blending naturally
- **NO 3D effects** — fully flat 2D
- **NO glow effects, neon, sparkles other than the one small flat sparkle**
- **NO Chinese characters anywhere in the image** — title is in the article, not the cover
- **NO English sentences, no subtitles, no taglines** — the only readable text in the image is the single numeral `7`
- **NO realistic book details** — no individual page edges, no stitching, no embossed leather textures
- NO people, NO devices, NO screens, NO robots
- The illustration must look like a clean SVG that could be exported from Figma, not like a digital painting

## Mood

Calm, intentional, friendly. A modern product handbook companion. Notion's signature: warm but minimal, a tiny bit hand-drawn, mostly geometric.
