# 封面 v3：Notion 插画风 · 减法时代

A clean **Notion-style flat illustration**, **16:9 aspect ratio**, editorial cover image.

## Style anchors

- Flat 2D vector illustration, slight hand-drawn imperfection in outlines
- Soft pastel palette: **cream beige `#F5EDE0`**, **soft sky blue `#B8D4E0`**, **muted peach `#F0C9A8`**, **warm grey `#9DA8B0`**, accent of **gentle gold `#D4A862`**
- Subtle paper grain / noise texture overlay (very light)
- Minimal whitespace-friendly composition, with a soft cream-paper background
- Friendly, calm, slightly nostalgic mood — like Notion's own marketing site illustrations
- No photorealism. No 3D. No glowing effects.

## Composition

A flat top-down desk view, slightly tilted (like Notion's flat-lay illustrations). Two main objects centered horizontally on a cream paper background.

**Left object — "the old way" (slightly faded, smaller):**
- A thick, weathered hardcover book illustrated in flat style
- Color: muted dusty brown `#8B6F4E`, with a slightly skewed proportion (taller than wide)
- A simple line-art tag sticking out the top reading `OLD STACK`
- A few floating tiny squiggle marks above (representing dust/age)

**Right object — "the new way" (brighter, clearer, the focal point):**
- A slim cream-white booklet, clean flat design
- A circular **gold seal** stamped in the center with the numeral `7` (large, bold, gold-on-cream)
- Below the seal, a single line of clean text: `減法時代` (or `Prompt 减法时代`) — make sure it's two crisp Chinese characters or short string, no garbled glyphs
- Tiny English subtitle below: `A New Playbook` (small, grey, sans-serif)

**Connecting element:**
- A simple flat arrow `→` between the two objects, drawn in soft grey `#9DA8B0`, slightly curved/hand-drawn

**Decoration:**
- A few tiny floating accent shapes around the booklet: small dots, a tiny star, a small `+` mark — Notion-illustration accents, very minimal
- A subtle horizontal cream baseline below the objects suggesting a desk surface

## Strict rules

- No realistic photography textures, no leather, no wood grain
- Use **flat illustration** style only
- All text must be perfectly legible — no glitched glyphs, no fake Chinese, no garbled English
- No screens, no devices, no robots, no people
- No purple gradients, no neon, no 3D
- Background stays cream/off-white, never dark

## Mood

Like opening a fresh, friendly product handbook. Calm, intentional, "we made this for you." The vibe of Notion's own homepage hero illustrations.
