Editorial illustration style. Clean, modern, slightly dark.

A visual metaphor for AI disrupting traditional employment. Split composition:

Left side: rows of identical office worker silhouettes at desks — orderly, uniform, the mass-employment economy.

Right side: a single glowing neural network / AI brain symbol that sends out automated threads/connections that reach each desk, one by one, as the human figures fade or dissolve.

The transformation is in progress — some workers are still there, some are already gone, some are mid-transformation.

Color: Predominantly dark navy/charcoal background. Human workers in warm sepia/amber. AI elements in cool blue-white light. The contrast between warm human and cool AI is intentional.

Style: Modern editorial magazine illustration. Bold, graphic, slightly ominous but not apocalyptic. Clean vector-meets-painterly aesthetic. The kind of image you'd see in The Economist or MIT Technology Review.
