# Composition
Type composition:
- Conceptual: Abstract shapes representing the relationship between human oversight and automated workflows. Clean information hierarchy with generous whitespace. Clear zones for visual and text elements.

Visual composition:
- Main visual: A simplified human silhouette (minimal, no facial features) seated at a desk, viewing a large monitor. On and around the monitor: floating task cards, checkbox lists, data flows, and progress indicators — all in motion, auto-completing. The person is calm and still, observing. This contrast (active system, still human) is the core tension.
- Layout: Human figure positioned center-left occupying about 40% width. Monitor and floating workflow elements occupy right side. Title "盯盘人" positioned top-left or bottom-left in handwritten style. Generous whitespace above.
- Decorative: Thin hand-drawn lines connecting workflow nodes; small checkbox icons (☑) scattered around the monitor area; subtle grid or dot pattern in background (very light); a few floating card shapes suggesting automation.

Color scheme:
- Background: white or very light warm gray (#FAFAF8)
- Primary ink: deep charcoal black (#1A1A1A)
- Accent: medium gray (#888888) for secondary elements and shadows
- No other colors — pure mono hand-drawn aesthetic

Rendering notes:
- Hand-drawn line art quality: slightly imperfect, organic lines (not perfectly straight)
- Varying line weights: thicker for the human figure, thinner for workflow elements
- Slight texture/grain on background to feel like paper
- No fills except light gray washes for depth
- Elements feel sketched, like Notion-style illustrations
- Clean and editorial, not rough or messy

Type notes:
- Conceptual cover: strong visual metaphor communicates the article's core idea immediately
- The automation elements (cards, checkboxes, flows) make the "system running itself" concept visible
- Human silhouette must be simple, non-realistic — just enough to show "a person watching"

Palette notes:
- Mono: strict black and white with grays
- No color accents
- High contrast for print and screen readability
- Elegant, timeless quality
