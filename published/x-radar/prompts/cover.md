# Cover Image Prompt

A cinematic, modern tech-themed cover image for an article about X Radar — an open-source tool that filters Twitter/X noise into a curated daily briefing.

**Central visual metaphor**: a radar sweep transforming scattered, chaotic tweet bubbles (noise) on the left into a clean, ordered vertical stack of highlighted "signal" cards on the right — visualizing the "scroll → read" transformation.

**Composition**:
- Left 40%: a dense, semi-transparent cloud of small tweet-like bubbles, chat fragments, short text lines, and @ symbols, drifting and overlapping — conveying information overload. Low opacity, muted.
- Center: a glowing circular radar sweep with concentric rings, a rotating scan line in bright cyan/violet, pulling attention inward. Subtle grid overlay.
- Right 40%: 3–4 crisp, elevated rectangular "digest cards" stacked vertically, each with a bright accent line, highlighted title bar, and a tiny avatar circle. Clean, organized, glowing edges.
- Connection: thin light trails arc from the noisy left cloud, through the radar center, condensing into the clean right-side cards.

**Palette**: deep midnight navy (#0a1530) and cosmic violet (#1a0b3a) background, cyan (#5ec3ff) and electric violet (#a78bfa) accents, warm white highlights (#f5f7ff) for the digest cards.

**Style**: flat-vector + subtle digital glow. Crisp geometry, subtle gradient shading, no realistic photography, no humans. Slight scanline / constellation-dot texture in the background for a "data in space" feel.

**Title placement**: title "X Radar" in large bold display sans-serif across the upper-left quadrant, with subtitle "把 X 精选，每天早晚送到邮箱" in smaller clean sans below it. Title in warm white with a subtle violet glow.

**Aspect ratio**: 16:9 cinematic.

**Mood**: bold, high-contrast, confident, quietly futuristic.

**Must NOT include**: realistic people/faces, X/Twitter logo directly (use abstract bubbles instead to avoid trademark), any Chinese-character watermark beyond the subtitle.
