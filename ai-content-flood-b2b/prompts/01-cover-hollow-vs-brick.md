# Cover concept: hollow infographics swept away, a solid brick revealed

A Notion-style flat editorial illustration, hand-drawn feel with slightly irregular
imperfect outlines, simple geometric shapes, generous whitespace (50-60% breathing room),
subtle warm paper-grain texture.

Background: warm off-white paper, color #FAF7F0, evenly lit, calm.

Central metaphor (single clear visual):
- On the LEFT and upper area: a loose stack / fan of thin, glossy, EMPTY infographic cards
  drifting and being swept away — each card is a hollow shell: faint thin mist-blue (#9CB4CC)
  borders, tidy empty color blocks, little arrows and bars that mean nothing, slightly
  translucent and weightless, a few tilting and falling off like discarded paper.
- A simplified hand silhouette (flat, no realistic skin, ink-black #0E1116 outline) sweeping
  these hollow cards aside with a brushing motion.
- Revealed UNDERNEATH at the lower-center: ONE solid, heavy, matte BRICK sitting firmly and
  grounded — clearly weighty and real, the opposite of the floating shells. The brick is the
  anchor of the composition.

Color system (brand, strict):
- Background off-white #FAF7F0
- Main outlines / the hand / brick edges: ink-black #0E1116 (NOT pure black)
- The solid brick carries a phosphor-green #1AB87C accent glow / a small green signal mark on
  top — green = the one thing that still has value
- Secondary elements / graphite labels: #3A4151
- Hollow shell card borders: thin mist-blue #9CB4CC
- Soft shadows / supporting blocks: brick-grey #6E6259
- NO orange, NO coral, NO purple gradient, NO neon, NO blue-teal tech look

Composition: visual anchor (the brick) lower-center or slightly offset; hollow cards flying
off toward upper-right; lots of empty warm space around. Editorial, warm, friendly, a little
wry. NO realistic humans (only a simplified flat hand silhouette). NO headline text.

Watermark: bottom-right corner, small (~6-7% of image width), text "雷码工坊笔记" in a clean
sans-serif matching the illustration, color graphite #3A4151 at ~60% opacity, with a small
3-4% margin from edges. Chinese glyphs MUST render correctly, no garbled characters, no
fake-looking CJK, correct kerning.

Aspect ratio 16:9.
