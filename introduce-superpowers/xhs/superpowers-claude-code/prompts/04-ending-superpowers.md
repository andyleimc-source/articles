# Xiaohongshu Ending Card — Notion Style (Sparse Layout)

Create a Xiaohongshu ending card in Notion minimalist hand-drawn style. Match the visual style of the reference image.

## Canvas
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait)
- Background: warm off-white #FAFAFA

## Layout: Sparse
Minimal content, maximum impact. One big idea, generous whitespace.

## Content Structure

### Top illustration
A simple hand-drawn balance scale or hourglass — one side labeled "慢一点" (go slower), other side labeled "少返工" (less rework). Arrow pointing to result below.

### Center — Big Quote
Very large bold text centered:
"慢即是快"

### Below quote — Data comparison
Two hand-drawn boxes side by side:
Left box (light red tint):
以前
30 分钟 × 返工 3 次
= 2 小时

Right box (light green tint):
现在
90 分钟 × 返工 0 次
= 90 分钟

Arrow: 省了 30 分钟！

### Bottom — CTA section
Hand-drawn rounded rectangle button:
"👇 评论区留言，发你安装命令"

Small text below:
GitHub: obra/superpowers

## Style Details
- Match reference image style throughout
- "慢即是快" in very large bold black, with hand-drawn circle-mark decoration
- Data boxes have hand-drawn borders, slight wobble
- The contrast is the key: red (before) vs green (after)
- Orange #E9963E accent on the scale/balance illustration
- CTA button: dark charcoal background, white text
- No watermark
