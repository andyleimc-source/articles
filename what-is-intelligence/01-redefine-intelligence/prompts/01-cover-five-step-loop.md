# Cover Image Prompt — 重新定义智能

A Notion-style editorial flat illustration for a Chinese tech essay cover, 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Composition**: Centered on a large circular loop diagram, the loop has 5 evenly spaced nodes arranged clockwise from the top. Each node is a small circle connected by curved arrows showing flow direction. The loop occupies about 55% of the canvas, sitting slightly to the left of center, with the title placed in the upper-right negative space.

**The 5 nodes (clockwise from top)**:
1. Top — small dotted/outlined circle, labeled "元模式" in tiny Chinese characters below (light graphite color)
2. Upper-right — small dotted/outlined circle, labeled "世界模型"
3. Lower-right — small dotted/outlined circle, labeled "预测"
4. Lower-left — **filled solid phosphor-green circle (#1AB87C)** with a soft glow, labeled "行动"
5. Upper-left — **filled solid phosphor-green circle (#1AB87C)** with a soft glow, labeled "反馈"

The visual contrast is critical: the first 3 nodes (top arc, "knowing") are light, hollow, almost ghostly. The last 2 nodes (bottom arc, "doing") are bright, solid, alive. The curved arrows between nodes are thin ink-pen strokes with slight hand-drawn wobble.
